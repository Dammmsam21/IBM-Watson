# 08 — Bloomberg (Bloomberg Terminal)

## What it is
A premium **real-time financial-markets, news, and analytics** system used across finance — accessed through the **Bloomberg Terminal** (and its function codes).

## Who owns it / current name
**Bloomberg L.P.** The product is the **Bloomberg Terminal** (a.k.a. "Bloomberg Professional Services").

## What you use it for
Real-time and historical **market data** (equities, fixed income, FX, commodities), **company financials**, **Bloomberg News**, analytics, and powerful screening — plus a built-in messaging network used by professionals.

## Key features to name in an interview
- **Real-time market data & pricing** across virtually all asset classes.
- **Bloomberg News** and deep historical data.
- Company analysis functions and financial statements; **function-code** navigation (e.g., a company "equity" page, news, financial analysis screens).
- **Screening / EQS-style equity screening**, charting, and analytics.
- Bloomberg **Messaging (the "chat")** — a de-facto professional network in finance.

> You don't need to memorize function codes for this role, but knowing Bloomberg is *navigated by typing function commands* shows familiarity.

## How *this* role uses it
- **Up-to-the-minute company & market data** for target profiles and partner briefings.
- **Macro / market context** to frame industry narratives in decks.
- **Cross-checking** financials and news found in other tools.

> Practical note: Bloomberg Terminals are expensive and seat-limited, so in a research team you may use them selectively alongside Capital IQ, LSEG Workspace, and Factiva rather than as the everyday tool.

## Typical tasks / questions it answers
- "What's the latest market data and news on this public company?"
- "How has this sector's index performed this year?"
- "Pull current financials and key metrics for Company X."

## Say-this-in-the-interview summary
> "The Bloomberg Terminal is the premium real-time markets, news and analytics system, navigated through function codes and covering every asset class plus Bloomberg News. In a research role I'd use it for current company and market data and macro context to support target profiles and partner briefings, complementing tools like Capital IQ, LSEG Workspace and Factiva."

# 06 — S&P Capital IQ (Capital IQ Pro)

## What it is
A **deep financial-data and analytics platform** for public and private companies — financials, valuations, comparables, screening, transactions, and ownership.

## Who owns it / current name
Owned by **S&P Global Market Intelligence**. The modern platform is **S&P Capital IQ Pro** (the classic desktop was "Capital IQ").

## What you use it for
Detailed **company financials and modeling inputs**, building **comparable-company ("comps") sets**, **screening** for companies that meet criteria, and researching **private equity / private companies, deals, and ownership**.

## Key features to name in an interview
- Standardized **financial statements & ratios** for millions of public **and private** companies, with history.
- **Screening** — filter the universe by financial, industry, geographic, and ownership criteria.
- **Comparable analysis / comps** and valuation multiples.
- **Transactions** (M&A, financings) and **18,000+ private-equity firms** with portfolio-company lists.
- Ownership, key developments, credit data, Excel plug-in for pulling data into models.

## How *this* role uses it
- **Financial profiling** of target companies for research decks (revenue, profitability, growth, leverage).
- **Screening to find targets** that match the A&A ideal-client profile (e.g., size, sector, ownership type).
- **Private-company insight** — useful since many audit targets are private.
- **Peer/competitor sets** for benchmarking a target against its industry.

## Capital IQ vs. Thomson/LSEG vs. PitchBook (be ready)
- **Capital IQ & LSEG Workspace** overlap a lot (both cover public financials, deals, estimates). Capital IQ is especially strong on **private companies, comps, and screening**.
- **PitchBook** is more specialized in **private capital markets** (VC/PE/M&A deal granularity, cap tables, valuations of startups).

## Typical tasks / questions it answers
- "Screen for US private companies in industrials with \$1–5B revenue."
- "Build a comps table for Company X against five peers."
- "What is this private company's financial profile and ownership?"
- "Which PE firm owns this target, and what else is in their portfolio?"

## Say-this-in-the-interview summary
> "S&P Capital IQ — now Capital IQ Pro from S&P Global — is a financial-data and analytics platform covering public and private companies, with strong screening, comparables and private-company and PE coverage. In this role I'd use it to build financial profiles of targets, screen for new prospects that fit our client profile, and benchmark companies against peers."

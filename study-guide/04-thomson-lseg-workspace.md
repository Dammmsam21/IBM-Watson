# 04 — Thomson (Thomson ONE → LSEG Workspace)

## What it is
A **financial-markets database** for company fundamentals, filings, analyst estimates, deals/transactions, and market data.

## Who owns it / current name (lots of history — know the chain)
The product the JD calls **"Thomson"** is **Thomson ONE**. Its lineage:

**Thomson ONE → Thomson Reuters Eikon → Refinitiv Eikon / Refinitiv Workspace → LSEG Workspace** (London Stock Exchange Group).

So today the modern equivalent is **LSEG Workspace** (formerly Refinitiv Workspace/Eikon), and **Datastream** is the historical time-series database in the same family.

> **Interview tip:** Saying "Thomson ONE is now LSEG Workspace, formerly Refinitiv" demonstrates current market awareness.

## What you use it for
Pulling **company financials and SEC/regulatory filings**, **analyst estimates (I/B/E/S)**, **M&A and capital-markets deal data with league tables**, ownership, ESG scores, pricing, and screening.

## Key features to name in an interview
- Public-company **fundamentals + links to filings** (10-K, 10-Q, proxies).
- **I/B/E/S** consensus and forward analyst **estimates**; analyst research reports.
- **Deals & league tables** — M&A, equity and debt issuance.
- **Datastream**: deep historical time-series (decades of data across markets and economies).
- ESG data, ownership/holders, officers & directors, key corporate events.
- Powerful **screening and charting**.

## How *this* role uses it
- **Financial snapshots** for target-company profiles (revenue, margins, growth, key ratios).
- **Filings & corporate events** to understand a company's situation and timing (e.g., fiscal year-end → audit timing).
- **Deal context** — has the target done acquisitions, IPOs, or refinancings that create audit needs?
- **Ownership & officers** to round out the picture of who controls/runs the company.

## Typical tasks / questions it answers
- "What are Company X's last 5 years of revenue, EBITDA and key ratios?"
- "Pull the latest 10-K / proxy for this company."
- "What analyst estimates exist for next year?"
- "Show recent M&A deals in this sector and who advised them."

## Say-this-in-the-interview summary
> "Thomson ONE — now LSEG Workspace, formerly Refinitiv Eikon — is a financial database covering company fundamentals, filings, analyst estimates, deals and league tables, ownership and ESG, with Datastream for deep historical data. In this role I'd use it to build the financial section of target profiles and to understand corporate events that signal audit opportunities."

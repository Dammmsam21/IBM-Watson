# 03 — D&B Hoovers (formerly "Hoovers")

## What it is
A **sales-intelligence / B2B prospecting platform** built on Dun & Bradstreet's huge commercial database. It turns company data into target lists and rich company profiles.

## Who owns it / current name
Originally **Hoover's** (founded 1990); acquired by **Dun & Bradstreet** (in 2003) and now branded **D&B Hoovers**. The JD's "Hoovers" = today's **D&B Hoovers**.

## What you use it for
Identifying and prioritizing **prospect companies**, then pulling a deep, structured profile of each one for research and outreach.

## Key features to name in an interview
- Built on the **Dun & Bradstreet Data Cloud** — **170M+ business records** (some sources cite far higher coverage), anchored by the unique **D-U-N-S Number** identifier.
- A typical company profile bundles **~40 reports**: company summary, contacts, **competitors**, **corporate family tree**, financials, industry insight, news, and **technologies in use**.
- **FirstResearch** industry reports covering 200+ industries (great for fast industry context).
- Advanced list-building / segmentation and **CRM/marketing integrations** (Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics 365, Marketo, etc.).
- "Trusted by 90% of the Fortune 500" is the common marketing line — handy context.

## How *this* role uses it
- **Targeting & prioritization:** Build and refine the list of companies the A&A Growth team should pursue.
- **One-stop company profile:** Quickly assemble the firmographics, financial snapshot, family tree, and competitor set for a target profile slide.
- **Industry primers:** Use FirstResearch to get up to speed on an unfamiliar sector before building a deck.
- **Maintain the targeting database** the JD explicitly mentions.

## Hoovers vs. OneSource (be ready for this)
Both are now Dun & Bradstreet assets and overlap heavily. D&B Hoovers is the flagship, modern UI; OneSource capabilities were merged in after the 2017 Avention acquisition. If asked "what's the difference," the honest, smart answer is: *"They've converged — both are now D&B sales-intelligence tools; D&B Hoovers is the current flagship."*

## Typical tasks / questions it answers
- "Build a list of target companies matching our ideal client profile."
- "Give me a full profile of Company X: size, financials, competitors, corporate family, key contacts."
- "What's a quick industry overview of the medical-devices sector?"

## Say-this-in-the-interview summary
> "D&B Hoovers is Dun & Bradstreet's B2B sales-intelligence platform, built on their Data Cloud and D-U-N-S identifiers. Each company profile bundles dozens of reports — firmographics, financials, competitors, corporate family and contacts — plus FirstResearch industry overviews. In this role it's my main tool for building and maintaining the target-company database and generating profile slides for partners."

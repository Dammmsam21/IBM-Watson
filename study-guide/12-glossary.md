## One-line "what is it" for each tool

- **Factiva** — global news & business-news aggregator.
- **D&B Hoovers / OneSource** — company firmographics, contacts, and prospect lists (sales intelligence).
- **LSEG Workspace (Thomson)** — financial fundamentals, filings, estimates, deals.
- **BoardEx** — board/executive profiles + relationship mapping ("who knows who").
- **Capital IQ** — public & private financials, comps, screening, PE.
- **PitchBook** — private markets: VC, PE, M&A deals.
- **Bloomberg** — real-time markets, news, analytics (Terminal).
- **IBISWorld** — industry research reports & benchmarks.

## Business / finance terms

- **Firmographics** — descriptive company attributes (industry, size, revenue, location, employees).
- **D-U-N-S Number** — Dun & Bradstreet's unique 9-digit company identifier.
- **Corporate family tree** — parent/subsidiary/branch hierarchy of a company.
- **Comps (comparable company analysis)** — valuing/benchmarking a company against similar peers.
- **Screening** — filtering a universe of companies by chosen criteria.
- **I/B/E/S** — Institutional Brokers' Estimate System; consensus analyst estimates (in LSEG Workspace).
- **League tables** — rankings of advisors/banks by deal volume/value.
- **M&A** — mergers and acquisitions.
- **PE / VC** — private equity / venture capital.
- **Cap table** — capitalization table; who owns what equity in a (usually private) company.
- **ESG** — environmental, social, governance data/criteria.
- **Trigger event** — a change at a company that creates a sales/BD opportunity.

## Audit / professional-services terms

- **Audit** — independent opinion on whether financial statements are fairly stated.
- **Assurance** — services that increase confidence in information (e.g., ESG assurance).
- **Big Four** — Deloitte, PwC, EY, KPMG.
- **Audit committee** — board subcommittee overseeing financial reporting & the auditor.
- **CFO / Controller** — key finance decision-makers (and audit buyers).
- **Auditor rotation / re-tender** — periodic change of auditor; a major BD trigger.
- **Independence** — the auditor's required separation from the client.

## BD / research terms

- **Secondary research** — using existing/published data (this whole role).
- **Competitive intelligence** — analysis of markets/competitors/targets.
- **Target / prospect** — a company the firm wants to win.
- **Pursuit** — organized effort to win a specific opportunity.
- **Pursuit materials / collateral** — decks, profiles, proposals used to pursue.
- **Pipeline** — set of active opportunities.
- **Storyboarding** — planning a deck's narrative before building slides.
- **Executive summary / BLUF** — the bottom line stated up front.

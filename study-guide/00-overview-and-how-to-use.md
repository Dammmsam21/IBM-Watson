# Study Guide — Tools for the Deloitte A&A "Strategic Relationship Management" Role

**Role:** Associate Consultant, Strategic Relationship Management — Audit & Assurance (A&A) Growth, Deloitte USI (Hyderabad)
**Requisition:** 355084
**What the job really is:** You support the US-based A&A Growth team by doing **secondary research** (desk research) on target companies, industries and executives, then turning that into **client-ready PowerPoint/Word/Excel deliverables** that help partners build relationships and win audit work.

This study guide breaks the role's required tools into bite-sized lessons so you can walk into the interview (and the job) confident about *what each tool is, why it exists, and how you'd actually use it here.*

## The 30-second mental model

Group the nine databases into **four buckets**. If you remember the buckets, you can reason about any question.

1. **News & narrative** → *Factiva* (and Bloomberg news). "What is being said about this company?"
2. **Company firmographics & prospecting** → *D&B Hoovers, OneSource*. "Who is this company, how big, who do I contact?"
3. **Financials, deals & markets** → *Capital IQ, Thomson/LSEG Workspace, Bloomberg, PitchBook*. "Numbers, valuations, M&A, ownership."
4. **People & relationships / industry context** → *BoardEx* (people/connections) and *IBISWorld* (industry context).

Audit & Assurance business development = **find the right target companies → understand them → find a warm path to the decision-makers → package it for a partner.** Every tool above plugs into one of those four steps.

# 02 — OneSource

## What it is
A **company, contact, and market-data platform** used for sales and business-development (BD) intelligence — i.e., building lists of target companies and finding the right people to contact.

## Who owns it / current name (important!)
OneSource was made by **Avention**. **Dun & Bradstreet acquired Avention in early 2017 (~$150M)** and folded the capabilities into its sales-acceleration line. In practice, OneSource's functionality now largely lives inside **D&B Hoovers / Dun & Bradstreet's Data Cloud**.

> **Interview tip:** If asked about OneSource, show you know it's now part of Dun & Bradstreet. That signals you actually understand the market, not just a list of names.

## What you use it for
Pulling **firmographics** (industry, size, revenue, locations, corporate family tree) and **contacts** for a company, and building targeted prospect lists for outreach.

## Key features to name in an interview
- Company profiles: industry codes, revenue/employee estimates, hierarchy (parent/subsidiary), and competitors.
- Contact data for decision-makers (titles, sometimes direct details), useful for BD outreach.
- List-building and segmentation by industry, geography, size, etc.
- Integrations with CRM/marketing systems (a hallmark of the D&B sales-acceleration suite).

## How *this* role uses it
- **Build the target universe:** Generate lists of companies that fit Deloitte A&A's ideal-client profile (e.g., by industry, revenue band, location).
- **Identify decision-makers:** Find CFOs, audit-committee chairs, controllers — the people a partner needs to reach.
- **Enrich the targeting database:** Feed firmographic fields into the Excel targeting database you maintain in this role.

## Typical tasks / questions it answers
- "List all manufacturing companies in the US Midwest with revenue between \$500M and \$2B."
- "Who are the finance decision-makers at this company?"
- "What is this company's corporate family tree (parents/subsidiaries)?"

## Say-this-in-the-interview summary
> "OneSource is a company- and contact-data platform for sales/BD intelligence. It was Avention's product and Dun & Bradstreet acquired it in 2017, so today its capabilities sit inside D&B Hoovers and the D&B Data Cloud. I'd use it to build target-company lists, pull firmographics, and identify the right finance decision-makers to feed into our targeting database and outreach."

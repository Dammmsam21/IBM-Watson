# 01 — Dow Jones Factiva

## What it is
A global **news and business-information research tool** that aggregates content from thousands of sources into one searchable archive.

## Who owns it / current name
Owned by **Dow Jones & Company**. Branded **Dow Jones Factiva** (sometimes just "Factiva"). Same name as in the JD — no rebrand to memorize here.

## What you use it for
Finding **what has been published** about a company, person, industry, or topic — news articles, press releases, trade journals, and market reports — and setting up alerts so you don't miss developments.

## Key features to name in an interview
- Aggregates **~18,000+ sources across ~150+ countries** in many languages, including premium titles like **The Wall Street Journal**, the **Financial Times**, and **Reuters / Dow Jones / AP** newswires.
- Powerful search with filters by date, region, industry, company, and subject; supports boolean/advanced search operators.
- **Alerts / newsletters** — automatically email you new coverage on a saved query (great for tracking a target account).
- Company "snapshot"/Companies & Markets data and the ability to build peer comparisons.
- Source de-duplication and translation, so global research is manageable.

## How *this* role uses it
- **Target-company monitoring:** Set up alerts on Deloitte's prospective audit clients so the Growth team hears about leadership changes, earnings, lawsuits, M&A, or auditor-rotation news first.
- **Trigger events:** A CFO change, a restatement, or an auditor tenure milestone is a *reason to reach out* — Factiva surfaces these.
- **Briefing prep:** Pull recent news to brief a partner before a client meeting.
- **Industry/market color:** Add qualitative "what's happening in the sector" narrative to research decks.

## Typical tasks / questions it answers
- "What has been in the news about Company X in the last 90 days?"
- "Has this company announced a new CFO, an acquisition, or a regulatory issue?"
- "Give me recent coverage of audit/accounting issues in the pharma sector."
- "Set up a weekly digest on my 25 target accounts."

## Say-this-in-the-interview summary
> "Factiva is Dow Jones's global news aggregator — tens of thousands of sources including the WSJ and Reuters. In a business-development context I'd use it to monitor target companies for trigger events like leadership changes, M&A or regulatory news, and to build the qualitative narrative around a company or industry for partner briefings and research decks."

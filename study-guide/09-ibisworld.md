# 09 — IBISWorld

## What it is
An **industry market-research** provider. Where the other tools tell you about *companies*, IBISWorld tells you about *industries* — analyst-written reports with market size, trends, drivers, and benchmarks.

## Who owns it / current name
**IBISWorld**. Branded as in the JD (written "IBIS World").

## What you use it for
Getting a **fast, structured understanding of an industry**: how big it is, how fast it's growing, what drives it, who the major players are, profitability/cost benchmarks, and the outlook.

## Key features to name in an interview
- **Industry reports** covering **up to ~50,000 titles** across major developed markets.
- Standard report structure: industry **definition**, **market size & growth**, **key external drivers**, **major players & market share**, **key success factors**, **financial/cost-structure benchmarks**, **SWOT**, and **5-year forecasts**.
- Analyst-written insight layered on top of economic/government data (Census, etc.).
- Cost-structure benchmarks (expenses as a % of revenue) and performance benchmarks.

## How *this* role uses it
- **Industry primers:** Quickly get smart on a sector before researching companies in it or briefing a partner.
- **Storyboarding & context slides:** Provide the "state of the industry" backdrop in research decks (market size, growth, drivers, trends).
- **Benchmarking:** Compare a target company against industry cost/performance benchmarks.
- **Credibility:** Cite industry statistics and key success factors to make partner-facing materials authoritative.

## Typical tasks / questions it answers
- "How big is the US medical-device industry and how fast is it growing?"
- "What are the key drivers and risks for this sector over the next 5 years?"
- "Who are the major players and what are typical profit margins?"
- "Give me an industry overview slide for this deck."

## Say-this-in-the-interview summary
> "IBISWorld is an industry-research library — thousands of analyst-written reports with market size, growth, drivers, major players, key success factors, benchmarks and forecasts. In this role it's my fastest way to get smart on an industry and to build the credible market-context slides that frame our target research for partners."
